What is Crop Chat AI?

Crop Chat AI is a smart farming assistant that uses AI agents to provide expert advice on crop health, irrigation, and soil care.

Do I need to be a tech expert to use it?

Not at all! The app is designed with a simple interface so farmers can get expert-level advice with just a few inputs.

How accurate is the advice provided?

The app combines GPT-4 with live web search tools, giving up-to-date and context-aware recommendations from virtual experts.

Can I download the crop advice?

Yes! After the agents generate advice, you can download the full report in Markdown format for easy reference.
